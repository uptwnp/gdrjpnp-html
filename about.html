About Trident Panipat

Trident Panipat is a signature upcoming residential plotted and apartment development by Trident Group, strategically located in Sector 19A, Panipat, near NH-44. Spread over a wide expanse, the project offers customized residential plots and modern apartments, enabling buyers to build or choose their dream home according to their preferences.

Project Overview

Trident Panipat stands out for its prime location, excellent connectivity to Delhi, Chandigarh, and NCR, and a host of world-class amenities. The project features landscaped parks, a swimming pool, gym, clubhouse, children's play area, jogging tracks, and more, all designed to promote a healthy and active lifestyle. With flexible payment plans and RERA approval, Trident Panipat is an ideal choice for families and investors alike.

About Trident Group

Trident Group is a renowned real estate developer known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and timely delivery. With a strong presence across NCR and other key regions, Trident Group has delivered numerous successful projects, earning a reputation for excellence and trust in the industry. Their focus on sustainable development, customer satisfaction, and modern amenities sets them apart in the real estate sector.

Whether you are looking for a premium plot to build your custom home or a modern apartment with top-notch facilities, Trident Panipat offers a harmonious blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience in one of Haryana's fastest-growing cities.
